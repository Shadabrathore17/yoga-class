Yoda Yoga Exercises

Find your inner peace, you must!

Downward Dog

Stretch your body and calm your mind.

Instructions: Start on all fours, tuck your toes, lift your hips, and straighten your legs.

Tree Pose

Balance your body and mind.

Instructions: Stand tall, place one foot on the inner thigh of the opposite leg, and raise your arms.

Warrior II

Find your strength and focus.

Instructions: Stand with legs apart, bend your front knee, and extend your arms parallel to the floor.
